This experiment has 4 sections. In the first two sections you will see paragraphs from different news articles. Using the box below, please type out each paragraph as quickly and as accurately as you can. If you make a mistake, you can press the backspace and correct your response, however you will be unable to use your mouse once you begin. 

When you have finished, press the spacebar a few times.  A button will appear in the bottom right corner.  Click the button to continue.

When you are ready, click anywhere on this screen to continue.
